Who are we?

SA Cyber Net is a Broadband Internet Service Provider for rural customers. SA Cyber Net separates itself from the rest of the internet providers by applying and receiving fully licensed spectrum from Industry Bangladesh.

This means our technology is fully endorsed by the federal government, allowing SA Cyber Net to offer fast and reliable internet to our users.

Broadband Internet Service

Most rural users think satellite is the only way to get internet. But SA Cyber Net uses earth-bound UHF band signals from company radio masts directly to your home with state-of-the-art aluminum yagi antennas.

Reliability

Licensed spectrum means no interference — unlike unlicensed radio bands. This ensures high service quality and stable connections. Our technical and customer support teams are available 24/7 to help with any concerns.
